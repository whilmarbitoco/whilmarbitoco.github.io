Chronicle

Galactic
Eras

The history of the galaxy is divided into distinct eras, each marked by monumental shifts in power, the discovery of new star systems, and the eternal ebb and flow of the Force.

ERA-0125,000 - 1,000 BBY

The Old Republic

An ancient era defined by the legendary wars between the Jedi Order and the original Sith Empire across the galaxy.

ERA-02300 - 82 BBY

The High Republic

A golden age of peace and prosperity where the Jedi reached the height of their power and influence.

ERA-0332 - 19 BBY

Fall of the Jedi

The era of the Clone Wars, the rise of Darth Sidious, and the tragic execution of Order 66.

ERA-0419 - 0 BBY

Reign of the Empire

A dark time where the Galactic Empire consolidated its power through fear and the construction of the Death Star.

ERA-050 - 5 ABY

Age of Rebellion

The Galactic Civil War where a small band of rebels fought to restore freedom and the Jedi returned.

ERA-065 ABY - Present

The New Republic

The fragile reconstruction of democracy and the subsequent emergence of the First Order from the shadows.
