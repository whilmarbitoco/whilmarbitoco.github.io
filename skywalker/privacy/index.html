Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for archive access, including your Holo-ID and sector coordinates. No biometric data or Force sensitivity readings are stored without explicit consent.

2. Data Encryption

All transmission data is encrypted using military-grade RSA-4096 protocols used by the Rebel Alliance. Your session history is automatically purged upon disconnection from the Holonet node.

3. Third-Party Access

We do not share your location data with Imperial Remnant factions or Bounty Hunter guilds. Your information is protected under the New Republic Privacy Act of 5 ABY.

4. Cookie Droids

Small "cookie" droids may be used to enhance your browsing experience and remember your preference for Sub-Light or Hyperspace navigation modes.
